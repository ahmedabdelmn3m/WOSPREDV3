HOW TO USE

1. Set the Impact Time — this is when you want all rallies to hit the target castle simultaneously. Use UTC.

2. Add Leaders — enter each rally leader's name, their march time (how long their march takes), and their rally fill time (1 or 5 minutes).

3. Order matters — the LAST leader in the list is the suicide (arrives 1st). Leaders above it sync behind it.

4. Click Calculate — the timetable shows exactly when each leader should launch their rally.

5. Copy All — paste directly into your alliance Discord or in-game chat.

EXAMPLE

Leader Alpha — march 1:15 — sends at 14:28:45 → hits at 14:35:00
Leader Bravo — march 3:08 — sends at 14:26:52 → hits at 14:35:00
Suicide (1st) — march 0:45 — sends at 14:29:15 → hits at 14:34:59

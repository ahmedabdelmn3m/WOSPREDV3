War Decision Machine

The next predictor layer treats scout screenshots as evidence, not perfect truth. The app should record what is visible, what OCR guessed, what you confirmed, and what remains hidden before recommending a hit, rally, hero swap, or avoid call.

Frontend source /frontend Update strategy-update-v1

Main Flow

  1. Upload own stats, enemy scout, hero lineup, formation, and optional battle report screenshots.
  2. Run OCR and capture values with confidence scores.
  3. Review uncertain or high-impact fields before prediction.
  4. Build battle input from visible stats, confirmed values, and hidden-risk assumptions.
  5. Show an outcome band, confidence level, main risks, and recommended action.

Scout Types

City scoutUse for city hits, city rallies, zeroing decisions, trap checks, and reinforcement checks.
Tile scoutUse only for exposed march or tile-hit decisions, not full city-defense prediction.
Battle reportBest for calibration because it shows real performance after hidden stats apply.

Hidden Risk

Scout reports can miss research, chief gear, charms, hero gear, pets, alliance tech, city bonuses, skins, temporary buffs, and reinforcements.

The predictor should reduce confidence when these unknowns can swing the fight.

OCR Review Rules

Any OCR field below 0.85 confidence should be reviewed. High-impact fields should be reviewed even when OCR confidence looks high.

⚔️ WOS Rally Timing Calculator

Synchronize your rally launches for perfect hit timing

📖 How to Use

  1. Add Leaders: Enter each leader's name and march time (how long it takes for troops to reach the target)
  2. Set Hitting Order: Choose the order you want leaders to hit (e.g., Alpha first, then Beta, then Gamma)
  3. Set Hit Spacing: Enter how many seconds apart you want each hit (0 = all hit at the same time)
  4. Calculate: Click "Calculate Launch Timing" to get the launch sequence
  5. Follow Instructions: Each leader follows their wait instruction to achieve perfect timing
